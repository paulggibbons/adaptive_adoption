\*\*Framework Overview:\*\*

Adaptive Adoption™ moves beyond linear change management ("The Culture Change Ripoff") to an evidence-based, complex-adaptive approach. It treats AI adoption not as a project to be managed, but as a capability to be grown—more like carpentry than calculus.



---



\## 1. Master the Craft

\*\*Theme:\*\* Moving from passive consumption (training) to active experimentation (mastery).



| Tools (The "Hardware") | Process (The Rituals) | Behaviors (The Norms) | Skills (The Capabilities) |

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| \*\*"Sandbox" Environments\*\*<br>\*(Safe, walled-off data spaces to break things)\* | \*\*Hackathons \& Datathons\*\*<br>\*(Time-boxed, high-energy problem solving)\* | \*\*"Fail Loudly"\*\*<br>\*(Sharing errors/hallucinations without shame)\* | \*\*Prompt Engineering\*\*<br>\*(The new syntax of work; talking to the machine)\* |

| \*\*Prompt Libraries\*\*<br>\*(Internal "Github for Prompts" to share recipes)\* | \*\*Peer-to-Peer Learning\*\*<br>\*(Weekly 30-min "show \& tell" sessions)\* | \*\*Curiosity over Compliance\*\*<br>\*(Asking "What if?" instead of "Am I allowed?")\* | \*\*Algorithmic Auditing\*\*<br>\*(Critical thinking: Verifying outputs, spotting bias)\* |

| \*\*Micro-Learning Feeds\*\*<br>\*(TikTok-style internal clips)\* | \*\*Reverse Mentoring\*\*<br>\*(Digital natives coaching execs on workflow)\* | \*\*Working Out Loud\*\*<br>\*(Narrating the work process, not just the result)\* | \*\*Data Literacy\*\*<br>\*(Understanding the fuel that powers the engine)\* |

| \*\*Co-Pilot Assistants\*\*<br>\*(Embedded workflow tools)\* | \*\*Rapid Prototyping\*\*<br>\*(Build-Measure-Learn cycles)\* | \*\*Collaborative Intelligence\*\*<br>\*(Viewing AI as a teammate, not a replacement)\* | \*\*Digital Adaptability\*\*<br>\*(Unlearning old methods to embrace new workflows)\* |
