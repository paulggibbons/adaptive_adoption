# AI Adoption Friction Suite — Decisions

## Accepted for v0.1

- The product family is the **AI Adoption Friction Suite**.
- The Suite contains three connected tools: Audit, Mapper, and 90-Day Intervention Planner.
- The Audit ships first.
- The Audit assesses one named initiative or workflow, not enterprise-wide AI maturity.
- The Audit and Mapper are distinct tools using one shared taxonomy and data model.
- The Mapper reveals where friction sits across teams, functions, roles, workflow handoffs, and adoption stages; it is not merely the Audit averaged by department.
- The Planner converts the diagnosed pattern into a sequenced 90-day response.
- Maven teaches the method; the website hosts the interactive tools.
- The initial Audit stores data locally and transmits nothing to the server.
- The tool’s epistemic strength and the behavioral evidence behind a finding remain separate scales.

## Deferred

- server-side response storage and benchmarks
- organization accounts and multi-rater aggregation
- team licensing and authenticated workspaces
- longitudinal comparisons
- industry-specific item variants
- validated weighting or normative cut points
- automated AI interpretation of confidential organizational data
